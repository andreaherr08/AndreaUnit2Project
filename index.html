Climate Change

What is Climate Change?

Climate change is when the usual weather of a certain area changes.Weather change and climate change are different, climate change can take up to years and weather changes in only a few hours or even minutes.

This is an article from NASA

Who does it affect?

It affects us humans as well as animals, these animals are the polar bears, snow leopards, and much more. The animals are affected because of the huge drop on temperatures, sea ice loss, sea level rise, and heavy rainfall.
